Why It’s Special

Extraterrestrial peridots are many times older than diamonds, and formed inside big asteroids before there was even an Earth. Diamonds formed from decomposing organic matter subjected to high temperatures and pressures. Extraterrestrial peridots are better symbols of true eternity than diamonds, which also happen to be thermodynamically unstable and certainly aren’t “forever”.
