A new kind of jewelry.
A new kind of jewelry company.

We’re founded on a simple idea: reinvent jewelry. Our products are precious because the jewels are both beautiful and incredibly rare: they are extraterrestrial. These are cosmic gems.

Our CEO has a PhD in planetary science, a job that gives him the opportunity to hold pieces of the Moon, Mars, and other unearthly objects, like asteroids, in his hands. When he proposed, he gave his fiancée (not a planetary scientist and not thrilled about conflict diamonds) an engagement ring with an e-peridot gem. She loved her ring made of “star stuff” so much that he founded Cosmic Origin.

Now, our customers design their own jewelry with cosmic gems. E-peridots are more “forever” than diamonds, and our customers don’t have to worry about the unfair labor practices, environmental degradation, or conflict zones fueled by the mining of rare earth minerals.

We made a different choice. We’re excited about helping you with yours.
