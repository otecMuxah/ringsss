Important stuff

PLACING AN ORDER

When you add an item to your shopping bag, you may either continue shopping or proceed to the checkout. If you are not logged in when proceeding to the checkout, you will be prompted to do so. Checkout involves 4 simple steps: Billing, Shipping, Payment and Order Confirmation.

Once we have received your order, you will receive an email acknowledging the details of your order. This email is not an order confirmation but rather recognition that we have received your requested order.

ONCE AN ORDER HAS BEEN PLACED, WE MAY NOT BE ABLE TO MODIFY OR CANCEL YOUR ORDER. We will do everything we can, but once production has started, we will only be able to issue refunds minus expenses sustained. We reserve the right to decline your order in the event that we are unable to obtain authorization for payment

ALL OF OUR JEWELRY IS CUSTOM-MADE. Please note, that it will take approximately 8 weeks to make your item to order. This ensures its uniqueness. We guarantee delivery within 10 weeks, and offer either a full refund or a 25% discount if we fail to deliver within that timeframe.

PAYMENT

We currently accept payments by Visa, MasterCard, American Express or Paypal. Payment will be debited and cleared from your account upon order confirmation. In filling out the payment requirements, you confirm that you are an authorized user of the credit/debit card. All credit/debit cardholders are subject to validation checks and authorization by the card issuer.

If the issuer of the credit/debit card rejects authorization of payment to COSMIC ORIGIN, we will not be held liable for any delay or non-delivery.

SHIPPING

Each piece is made to order so please allow 8-10 weeks for your item (excluding shipping).

All orders ship with FedEx. We require a physical address, as FedEx does not ship to PO Boxes. Standard shipping and handling charges within the United States are included in the price.

Expedited shipping is available. Please email for rates.

We do ship internationally, however, there are absolutely NO returns or exchanges on international shipments.

Customers can track their shipments at www.fedex.com.

RETURN AND EXCHANGE POLICY

If you are unhappy with an item that you have purchased from us online, you may return it for store credit within 30 days of receipt. Items must be returned with their original packaging and in perfect, unworn condition.

To return an item, please email us at customerservice@cosmicorigin.com for a return authorization number. Items cannot be accepted for a return or repair without an RA number.

For your protection, please insure the package for the full purchase amount. Cosmic Origin is not responsible for items lost or damaged during transit.

Store credit is issued upon receipt of the item. Refunded credits do not include shipping costs.

SALES TAX

Cosmic Origin does not charge sales tax for orders delivered in the United States with the exception of deliveries in the state of New York, in which applicable sales tax will be added to the order amount.

WARRANTY / REPAIR POLICY

We offer repairs due to user wear, production fault, lost earring mates, or any other alteration to a piece of Cosmic Origin jewelry. We stand behind the quality of our merchandise and are happy to provide repairs due to breakage or damage free of charge for one year from the date of purchase on our online store. After one year, repairs may incur a charge. Elective repairs, such as ring resizing or chain lengthening, or errors by the customer typically involve a charge. They are repaired at the owner's expense plus the shipping charge.

For elective repairs there is a $25 service charge plus the cost of additional materials and shipping.

Half pairs of earrings cost half the price of a full pair of earrings plus shipping.

If an item needs to be repaired, please email us at customerservice@cosmicorigin.com for a return authorization number. Items cannot be accepted for a repair without an RA number. Please note that we are not responsible for your item until it is in our possession and that repairs typically require 4-6 weeks to complete.
